I was deep in a real project, on a roll, the assistant doing exactly what I needed. I pasted in one
more big chunk of work and hit enter.

Nothing. Then a timeout. Then, when it finally answered, it stopped mid-sentence, like someone
yanked the cord out of the wall.

::note
You probably assumed you did something wrong. Most people do.
::

Neither you nor the tool was broken. Something very predictable happened, and once you can see it,
you can work around it every single time.
