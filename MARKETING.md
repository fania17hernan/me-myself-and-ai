## What it is

A free, bilingual (English/Spanish) micro-course. Four levels, 26 bite-sized lessons (~7–10 min each), each built around a single sticky analogy and a quick 3-question check. Works on your phone; no app, no login, just a link.

- **Level 1 · Start Here**, how AI behaves, so it stops surprising you
- **Level 2 · The Tools**, what every button actually does (chats, projects, connectors, and which model to use)
- **Level 3 · Make It Yours**, customize AI to work the way you do
- **Level 4 · Under the Hood**, the "scary" tech words, finally demystified
- **Glossary**, 41 terms in plain words, searchable, any time

## Why it exists

Most people aren't bad at AI, they were handed the wrong instructions. Technical docs assume you're an engineer. "AI for business" courses are vague and forgettable. Neither shows you how it *actually works* in a way that sticks.

The spark was real: watching a sharp, accomplished professional, Director-level, college-educated, go blank the moment words like "tokens" or "context window" came up. Not because he couldn't get it. Because nobody had ever explained it in his language. He's the whole market.

So this teaches AI the way a smart friend would over coffee: tie every concept to something you already understand, a gas tank, a whiteboard, a confident intern, and the idea finally lands.

---

## The problem it solves

Capable professionals are quietly **intimidated and overwhelmed** by AI. They know it matters, they don't know what they're missing, and the jargon makes them feel behind. This closes that gap, not with more features, but with **understanding you can actually keep and explain to a coworker.**

## What's in it for you (the learner)

Lead with these. They're benefits, not features:

- **Stop feeling behind.** In about 10 minutes you'll understand a concept you can use today and explain tomorrow.
- **No more guessing.** Know why AI cuts off, forgets, or makes things up, and exactly what to do about it.
- **Get more out of the tools you already pay for** (or use the free ones better).
- **Talk the talk without faking it.** Walk into any AI conversation and hold your own.
- **Built for your job, not for coders.** Plain language, real-work examples, zero fluff.
- **Free, on your phone, in English or Spanish.** Start now, one lesson at a time.

## How to socialize it (simple plan)

1. **Start with 5–10 people you know**, one personal message each (use the outreach copy). Personal asks beat broadcasts. Your first tester is the person this was built for.
2. **Post one analogy a week** on LinkedIn, the gas tank, the whiteboard, the confident intern. Lead with the *insight*, link at the bottom. The analogy is the marketing.
3. **Put the link in your bio** everywhere (LinkedIn, Instagram, email signature).
4. **Repurpose into 30-second clips** for Reels/TikTok/Shorts, one analogy each. They're naturally visual.
5. **Use it as a door-opener** for consulting/network conversations: "I made this, take a look, then let's talk."
6. **Spanish network is a second launch**, not an afterthought, the course is already fully bilingual. Share the `/es/` link directly with Spanish-first contacts.

**Golden rule:** never sell the course, share the *insight*. If the analogy lands, people click. That's the funnel.
