Glossary

Every term, in plain words

Look something up. No lesson required.

Token
A chunk of a word. It's the unit AI reads and writes, roughly three-quarters of a word.
Learn this properly · 2 min →
Rate limit
A cap on how much you can use a tool in a given window. Like a gas station that only lets you fill up so many times an hour.
Learn this properly · 2 min →
Context window
How much the AI can hold in mind at once. Everything earlier in the conversation counts against it.
Learn this properly · 3 min →
Checked July 2026
Confabulation
When AI states something false with complete confidence. Not lying, which requires knowing the truth.
